About Kamil's Bicycle Hub

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Kamil Wozniak, Kamil's Bicycle Hub started as a small workshop driven by a deep passion for cycling and a vision to make quality bicycles accessible to everyone. What began as a humble operation in a garage, fueled by late nights and countless cups of coffee, quickly grew into a community-focused hub for cyclists of all levels. Kamil, a lifelong enthusiast, realized the need for a place where riders could not only find reliable bikes but also receive honest advice, expert service, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Over the years, we've expanded our offerings, cultivated strong relationships with top manufacturers, and built a team of dedicated professionals who share our commitment to excellence. Our journey has been about more than just selling bikes; it's about fostering a love for cycling, promoting healthy lifestyles, and building a vibrant local cycling community.

Our Mission

At Kamil's Bicycle Hub, our mission is to empower individuals to embrace the joy and freedom of cycling. We are committed to providing the highest quality bicycles, accessories, and maintenance services, ensuring every ride is safe, comfortable, and exhilarating. We believe that cycling is not just a mode of transport or a sport, but a way of life that connects people with nature, promotes well-being, and fosters a sense of adventure.

We strive to be a trusted resource for our customers, offering personalized guidance to help them find the perfect bike that matches their needs and aspirations. Through our dedication to exceptional service, community engagement, and sustainable practices, we aim to inspire more people to discover the transformative power of two wheels. Our ultimate goal is to see more smiles, more miles, and more memories created on bicycles.
